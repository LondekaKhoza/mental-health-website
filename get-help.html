Get Help Now

If you or someone you know needs immediate support, reach out to the numbers, emails, or addresses below. You are not alone.

  • National Crisis Helplines:
  • Gender-Based Violence Command Centre (GBVCC): 0800 150 150 | SMS: 31531
  • Childline SA: 0800 055 555
  • South African Police Service (SAPS) Emergency: 10111
  • National Substance Abuse Helpline: 0800 12 13 14
  • South African Depression and Anxiety Group (SADAG): 0800 567 567
  • Rape Crisis National Helpline: 021 447 9762
