🌿 Mental Health Tips

Gentle reminders to take care of your mind, body, and soul.

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink water regularly. Dehydration can make you feel tired and stressed.

🛏️ Get Enough Sleep

Try to rest for 7-9 hours. Your brain needs it to reset and heal.

🧘 Practice Mindfulness

Take a few minutes each day to breathe deeply and check in with yourself.

📵 Take a Break From Screens

Step away from your phone or computer to reset your focus and mood.

🤝 Reach Out

Talk to someone you trust. You don't have to carry everything alone.

🎨 Do Something You Enjoy

Read, draw, dance, or listen to music—whatever brings you joy!
